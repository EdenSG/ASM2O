About

Eden S.-G.

I am inspired by:

  • The Internet (as a concept, as well as as a global phenomenon)
  • Creative Commons, Open Source
  • People exploring what art and literature can be:
    • John Cage
    • Cory Doctorow
    • Brian Eno
    • Peter Schmidt
  • The Oblique Strategies
  • The I Ching

I value counterculture.

I respect questions. . . . ?

I enjoy collaboration.

I appreciate change and adaptation.

I try come to creativity with a blank slate - I like to think of my art as a constant improvisation. That said, I am always working off the work of others: I believe that everything is a remix in some way.

I try to live in the moment as I work, letting the process ebb and flow on its own. As a musician and a musical improviser, I attempt to bring the idea of exploration to my visual and multimedia art. Instead of going directly from A to B, I like to start at A, wander a little, find C, look around and see D, and trip over J to run into W. I start out not knowing where I'm going to end up.

I would like to bring empathy into more of the world. I want others to see what I do, to think about it, and be inspired to create something of their own, whatever that may be. I think that artists should be able to limit themselves if they want to, but that no one else should place limitations on us. I hope to give people an opportunity to step back from their daily life and look at the world on a larger scale.

I enjoy using technologies in ways that are not intended. I am a tinkerer, a creator, and a hacker. I love opposites; and opposites of opposites, different from the original.

Messing up is OK. So is completely failing. If you have something to show, great. if not, try something completely different.
